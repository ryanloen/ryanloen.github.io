The Seattle Business Review is excited to announce that nominations are now open for our annual 30 under 30 award. This prestigious award recognizes the top 30 young professionals under the age of 30 who are making a significant impact in their industry and the local community.

Do you know a colleague who deserves recognition for their hard work and achievements? Now is the time to nominate them for the 30 under 30 award! We are looking for individuals who are innovative, ambitious, and driven, and who are making a positive difference in their field.

To nominate a colleague for the 30 under 30 award, simply visit our website and fill out the nomination form. Be sure to provide detailed information about the nominee and why they are deserving of this award. Nominations will be accepted until January 1st 2023.

Don't miss this opportunity to showcase the talent and achievements of your colleagues! Nominate a deserving individual for the 30 under 30 award today.
