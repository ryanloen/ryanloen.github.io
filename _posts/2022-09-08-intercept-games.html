Intercept Games is a Seattle-based company that has made a big splash in the gaming world with their hit indie game, "Kerbal Space Program".

Founded in 2011 by a team of experienced game developers, Intercept Games set out to create a space exploration game that was both challenging and fun. After several years of development, "Kerbal Space Program" was released to critical acclaim, receiving numerous awards and accolades for its innovative gameplay and realistic physics engine.

"Kerbal Space Program" tasks players with building and launching their own spacecraft, navigating the dangers of space and completing a variety of missions. The game offers a high degree of realism, with players having to consider factors such as fuel consumption, aerodynamics, and gravitational forces in order to successfully complete their missions.

Despite its complex gameplay, "Kerbal Space Program" has been a commercial success, with a dedicated fan base and a steady stream of updates and expansions. In addition to its popularity among gamers, the game has also been used by NASA and other space agencies as a training and educational tool.

Intercept Games continues to operate out of its Seattle headquarters, working on new projects and updates for "Kerbal Space Program" as well as other upcoming games. The company's success is a testament to the thriving indie game development scene in Seattle, and a reminder of the city's reputation as a hub for innovation and creativity.
