Seattle startup, Xealth, is making waves in the digital health industry with its innovative platform for organizing and deploying digital health tools.

Founded in 2016 by a team of experienced healthcare and technology professionals, Xealth has developed a comprehensive platform that enables health systems to easily integrate and activate digital health tools. The platform is designed to help health systems improve patient outcomes, reduce costs, and streamline operations.

Xealth's platform is unique in its ability to support a wide range of digital health tools, including apps, devices, and services. This allows health systems to easily select and deploy the tools that are most relevant to their patients and clinical needs. In addition, the platform provides robust analytics and reporting capabilities, allowing health systems to track the effectiveness of their digital health initiatives and make data-driven decisions.

The company has already partnered with several major health systems, including Providence St. Joseph Health and the Mayo Clinic. These partnerships have demonstrated the effectiveness of Xealth's platform in improving patient outcomes and reducing costs.

Xealth is continuing to expand its operations, with plans to develop new features and integrations for its platform. The company's success is a testament to the growing importance of digital health solutions and the need for effective platforms to support their deployment and activation.
